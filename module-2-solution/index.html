"BIRYANI"

It is a dish that is popular worldwide, and it takes time and practice to make it. It is prepared with basmati rice mixed with various spices like clove, bay leaves, cardamom, and vegetables. The main components of Biryani are basmati rice, vegetables, and spices. Biryani can be made both with or without meat.
